Pink floyd - The Wall

Description.

"The wall" is a rock album that was given to us by Pink Floyd in 1979. The album was written by mostly the legend, Roger Waters.
In 1982 a movie was released (pink floyd: The Wall), the movie could clearly picture the concept behind the album with the help of Pink(the protagonist of the movie).
